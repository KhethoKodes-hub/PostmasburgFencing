Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical fencing project take?
The duration of a fencing project depends on the size and complexity. A standard residential fence can take 2-5 days, while larger commercial projects may take 1-3 weeks. We'll provide a timeline during our consultation.
Are you available for emergency plumbing services?
Yes, we offer emergency plumbing services for urgent issues like burst pipes, severe leaks, and blocked drains. Call us at 073 961 6084 for immediate assistance.
Do you provide free quotes?
Yes, we offer free, no-obligation quotes for all our services. Contact us to schedule an assessment and we'll provide a detailed estimate for your project.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve Postmasburg and the surrounding Northern Cape areas. For larger projects, we may travel to other locations - contact us to discuss your specific needs.
