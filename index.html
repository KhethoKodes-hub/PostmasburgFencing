Our Story

Established in 2012 and formally registered in 2014, Postmasburg Fencing emerged from recognizing the gap in quality services for emerging black SMMEs in our rapidly growing community.

Under the leadership of Mr. Andries Jacobs, who brings 12 years of plumbing expertise and 6 years in fencing, building, and paving, we've built our reputation on integrity, affordability, and community empowerment.

Our mission goes beyond business - we're committed to eradicating poverty, uplifting and empowering youth by employing skilled workers from Postmasburg communities.
